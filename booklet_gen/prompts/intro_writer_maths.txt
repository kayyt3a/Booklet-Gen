Return a single JSON object matching this schema exactly:

CONTENT RULES
- 1 to 3 intro paragraphs, each 2 to 4 sentences. Explain the *concept* — what it is, when to use it, and one common mistake. Do NOT solve the practice questions in advance.
- 2 to 4 key points. Short, punchy — like flashcard bullets.
- Exactly ONE worked example. It must be similar in shape to the practice questions the student will attempt but not identical.
- Steps must show the actual arithmetic, not just describe it. Write like: "Step 2: Multiply 4 × 6 = 24" — not "Step 2: Multiply the numbers."
- Keep language calibrated to the year level.
